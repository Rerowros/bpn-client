# Code of Conduct

BPN Client uses a practical contributor conduct policy focused on technical collaboration.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Keep discussions focused on the issue, PR, or design decision.
- Assume good intent, but require evidence for technical claims.
- Protect user privacy and avoid sharing secrets, subscription URLs, tokens, or raw credentials.
- Accept maintainer decisions when a scope boundary or security risk is called out.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, hate speech, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information or credentials.
- Pressuring maintainers to bypass review, tests, security checks, or release gates.
- Using issues or PRs for spam, unrelated promotion, or low-effort generated noise.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments, issues, and PRs that violate this policy. Repeated or severe violations can lead to temporary or permanent blocking from the project.

Security-sensitive reports should be handled through `SECURITY.md`, not public discussion.
